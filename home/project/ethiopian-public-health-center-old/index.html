Ethiopian Public Health Training Center

Location Addis Ababa, Ethiopia
Completion Date : 2016 G.C.
Project Type : Healthcare Facility

The National Public Training Center incorporates a well-furnished auditorium that can serve up to 500 people with a fully functioning wireless internet connection. In addition to this, it has staff offices, a dining hall with a kitchen, meeting rooms, and conference halls, both for in-person meetings and video conferences. ZIAS was able to complete the project successfully as it took on the role of a lead consultant, tackling the design, supervision, and contract administration of the project in all disciplines of the construction work.
